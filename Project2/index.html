The Changing Oceans

Global warming has caused many changes in our oceans in recent years and will continue to do so. Please scroll down to learn about the effects

Due to global warming, the oceans are changing at a rapid pace

Changing oceans cause: coral bleaching, fish migration, drowned wetlands, ocean acidification, and a positive feedback loop that increase a warming trend

Wildlife that calls the ocean home are already decreasing in startling numbers

If we make the health of our oceans a priority now, we can work together to save them!
